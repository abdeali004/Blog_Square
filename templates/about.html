About Blog Square

Blog Square is a blogging website where you can read, write, gain and share different types of information. The 'blog square' is named after a square of blogs. A 'square' is an open, typically four-sided area which connects multiple roads or path. Here the 'roads' refer to different areas of knowledge.

Blog Square is a place where different types of ideas from various people connect, and every single person can gain different types of knowledge.
